Pasta Fagiolo




What is it?

Pasta fagiolo, hereafter referred to as the classic "Pasta Fazool" is a super easy, inexpensive, and delicious meal that will hold you over for hours. It also has the benefit of having a fair amount of protein to help you build powerful, rounded glutes that may quiver with excitement at the thought of this delicious dish.


    Ingredients You'll Need

  • 1 can of white beans (Cannelloni beans are the best option, honestly)
  • 1 can of kidney beans
  • 2-3 carrots
  • 2-3 celery stalks
  • 1 large white onion
  • 1 box of chicken stock
  • 1/2 tube of tomato paste
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper, dried italian seasoning
