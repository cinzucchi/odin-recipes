Cheese Ramen


Go back

What is it?

Cheese ramen is super simple. It's cheese and ramen. Add an egg if you want.

Ingredients

  • Packet of Ramen
  • Slices of American Cheese
  • An egg

Instructions

  1. Boil ramen
  2. Add egg just before ramen is done. Mix the egg well with the noodles.
  3. Remove from heat. Add 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6 slices of cheese. Literally add as much as you want.
  4. Mix the ramen around so that the cheese is distributed equally throughout the dish
